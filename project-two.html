What do we find in the Galaxy?

A galaxy is a collection of stars and interstellar material held together by gravity

The galaxy our Sun lives in is called the Milky Way or the Galaxy

The key components of our Galaxy are;

  1. A bulge of old stars in the center
  2. A disk of stars and gas
  3. a halo of globular clusters
